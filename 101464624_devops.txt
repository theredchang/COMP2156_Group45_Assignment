COMP2156 DevOps covers topics, tools, and best practices in the application of DevOps toward better software development and deployment. Major topics concerning version control, Continuous Integration, and Continuous Deployment, Infrastructure as Code, IaC, Docker, Kubernetes in Containerization, and Cloud Computation will be described in this class. Students who learn hands-on by labs and projects will discover how to apply workflow automation; better collaboration for developers and with an operation team to improve the reliability, scalability, and security of software applications.
