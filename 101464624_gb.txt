George Brown College is a state college with different campuses in Toronto. 
It includes a variety of diploma, degree, and certificate courses that provide practical learning, industry partnerships, and career-oriented education to students. 
Courses are offered in business, technology, health sciences, hospitality, and many more at this institution. 
George Brown is great at putting much emphasis on employable skills and co-op opportunities that get students on the path to a successful career.
