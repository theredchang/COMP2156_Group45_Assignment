George Brown College was established in 1967 and is located in Toronto. It was named after George Brown, a Canadian politician who was also one of the Fathers of Confederation. The college reflects his strong belief in public education, with it now having approximately 30,000 full-time students. George Brown offers a bunch of different programs, such as construction, theatre, and technology to name a few. People from the GTA, all the way across Canada, and even around the world attend through online and in-person classes.
